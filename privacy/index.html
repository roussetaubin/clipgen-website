Privacy Policy – ClipGen

Last updated: May 2026

ClipGen is a personal productivity tool that helps content creators clip YouTube videos and publish them to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

ClipGen collects only the YouTube URLs you provide. No personal data is stored on third-party servers. Videos are processed locally on your device.

2. TikTok Data Usage

ClipGen accesses your TikTok account only with your explicit consent via OAuth 2.0. Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are never shared with third parties. We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish clips on your behalf, with your authorization.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties. Video clips are sent directly to TikTok via their official API only when you explicitly authorize it.

4. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time from your TikTok account settings. No data is retained on our servers.

5. Security

All OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings at tiktok.com/setting.

6. Children's Privacy

ClipGen is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For any questions: clipgen.app@gmail.com
